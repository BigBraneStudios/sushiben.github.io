INTRODUCTION

Big Brane Studios encourages fans to create and share content inspired by Sushi Ben and the world of Kotobuki Town. We enjoy seeing the creativity of our community and want to support it within clear and reasonable guidelines.

This Fan Content Policy outlines what is permitted when using Sushi Ben intellectual property.

"Big Brane Studios" means Big Brane Studios, Inc., a Delaware corporation, and its parents, subsidiaries, and affiliates.

"Sushi Ben IP" means all intellectual property owned by Big Brane Studios related to Sushi Ben, including but not limited to characters, names, locations, story elements, artwork, music, audio, video, logos, and other proprietary content.

"Fan Content" means original content created by you that incorporates or is based on Sushi Ben IP, including but not limited to artwork, videos, streams, mods, and other creative works.

LICENSE GRANT

Subject to your compliance with this Policy, Big Brane Studios grants you a limited, non-exclusive, revocable, non-transferable license to create and share Fan Content based on Sushi Ben IP.

This license does not grant any ownership rights in Sushi Ben IP and may be revoked at any time at Big Brane Studios' sole discretion.

PERMITTED USES (DO'S)

You may:

1. Create and share non-commercial Fan Content, subject to the restrictions below.
2. Stream Sushi Ben on platforms such as Twitch, YouTube, or similar services.
3. Upload gameplay videos, clips, and VODs to platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, or Twitch.
4. Monetize videos and streams through platform-supported features, including:
   - Advertising revenue
   - Platform partner programs
   - Subscriptions and donations

If you are unsure whether something is allowed, please contact us at:
https://www.sushiben.com/#contact

RESTRICTIONS (DON'TS)

You may not:

1. Sell, license, or otherwise commercially exploit Fan Content, including physical or digital merchandise, regardless of scale.
2. Mass-produce or commercially distribute Fan Content using Sushi Ben IP.
3. Run crowdfunding campaigns (e.g., Kickstarter, Indiegogo) to create or sell merchandise based on Sushi Ben IP.
4. Represent Fan Content as official, licensed, or endorsed by Big Brane Studios.
5. Use Sushi Ben IP in a way that is racist, transphobic, homophobic, defamatory, or otherwise harmful to individuals or groups.
6. Use Sushi Ben IP in a way that could reasonably harm the reputation of the Sushi Ben brand, including inappropriate or offensive depictions.
7. Use Sushi Ben IP in connection with religious or political messaging or imagery.
8. Use Sushi Ben IP to create or promote NFTs, cryptocurrencies, or blockchain-based assets.

REQUIRED DISCLAIMER

Fan Content must clearly and reasonably include the following disclaimer:

"This is unofficial fan content and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Big Brane Studios."

By creating and sharing Fan Content, you grant Big Brane Studios a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, display, reproduce, and share your Fan Content for promotional and business purposes.

- Request removal of any Fan Content that violates this Policy
- Revoke this license at any time, for any reason
- Take legal action where appropriate

Failure to comply with this Policy may result in enforcement actions, including content removal requests.

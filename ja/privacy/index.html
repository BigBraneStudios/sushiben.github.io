July 19, 2024

1. Scope

This document outlines the terms and conditions (the "Policy") that Big Brane Studios Inc. ("Big Brane", "we", "our", and their derivatives) uses to govern data and information collection, privacy rights and obligations for Sushi Ben (the "Game") and services related to the Game.

2. Data Collection and Use

2.1 Data is not collected from users in connection with game play and related activities.

2.2 Data may be collected from users through:

* external communication to Big Brane by users through means such as email, social media, and phone calls.
* direct communication to Big Brane by users through means such as surveys, bug reports, and online forms.
* third-party sales platforms by way of user interactions, purchases, and playtime.

2.3 Personal information that may be collected by Big Brane can include:

* first and last names,
* e-mail addresses,
* profile pictures,
* social media usernames and profiles,
* purchase history,
* game play history, and
* geographic location.

2.4 Big Brane does not actively collect this information from users. Users can decide whether to provide this information by opting to not engage with Big Brane or such third-party sales platforms and by making use of privacy settings available to users by third party sales platforms.

2.5 Big Brane may use information to:

* provide users with game support,
* improve game quality and user experience by way of troubleshooting and bug fixes,
* monitor and enforce legal terms and obligations, and
* monitor and prevent harmful or illegal activities.

2.6 Big Brane does not share personal information with third parties without user consent unless legal requirements or legal obligations dictate this is necessary.

2.7 If you think Big Brane may have collected data from a user under the age of 13 through communication to Big Brane or third-party sales platforms, please notify Big Brane at bigbranestudios@gmail.com and Big Brane shall take reasonable steps to have that data removed.

Big Brane may disclose personal information if required by law or valid requests by public authorities.

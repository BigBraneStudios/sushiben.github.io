Full Description

Welcome to Kotobuki Town, a quaint community currently being gobbled up acre by acre by dastardly big-city landsharks. It is also your new home. Bring the locals together, help small businesses fight back, and protect what makes this town special.

Featuring a gorgeous seaside setting, 3D manga panel storytelling, and a fully voiced cast of colorful characters, Sushi Ben blends heartfelt narrative moments with playful activities and memorable humor.

Gameplay + Story

  • Meet and bond with residents through character-driven quests.
  • Take on mini-activities including fishing, archery, and more.
  • Uncover local stories while building momentum to save the sushi bar.
  • Designed for cozy pacing with expressive visuals and strong performances.

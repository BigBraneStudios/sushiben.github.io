About The Game

Sushi Ben is a VR narrative adventure where you support a small town and its people while trying to help a local sushi bar survive. Inspired by Japanese storytelling and manga presentation, the game blends heartfelt character moments with light exploration and mini-games.

Highlights

Story-Driven Adventure

Meet quirky locals, build trust, and uncover what makes Kotobuki Town worth fighting for.

Manga-Inspired Presentation

Step into stylized scenes that blend comic aesthetics with immersive VR interaction.

Voice-Acted Characters

Enjoy performances that bring each character and their personal stakes to life.

Comfortable, Accessible Play

Designed for approachable VR sessions with clear interactions and a welcoming pace.

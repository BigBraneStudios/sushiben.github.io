1. Scope

This Privacy & Data Collection Policy (“Policy”) governs how Big Brane Studios, Inc. (“Big Brane,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information related to Sushi Ben (the “Game”).

2. Data Collection and Use

2.1 Big Brane does not collect personal data through gameplay. However, personal data may be collected through other interactions as described below.

2.2 Personal data may be collected when voluntarily provided by users through:
- direct communications such as email, support requests, surveys, or bug reports;
- public or private interactions on social media; and
- online forms or similar submissions.

2.3 Certain information may be collected and processed by third-party platforms or services, including storefronts, website hosts, video or music embeds, social platforms, and similar services. Big Brane does not directly collect all such data and may receive only limited information from such third parties when necessary to operate, maintain, support, or improve the Game or related services.

2.4 Personal information that may be collected includes:
- name;
- email address;
- social media identifiers; and
- profile information voluntarily provided by users.

2.5 We use personal information to:
- provide support;
- respond to inquiries;
- improve the Game and related services;
- enforce legal terms; and
- prevent harmful or illegal activity.

2.6 We do not sell personal information. We may disclose personal information:
- with your consent;
- to service providers or vendors acting on our behalf;
- to comply with legal obligations; or
- to protect rights, safety, or property.

2.7 The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal information based on:
- your consent;
- contractual necessity;
- legitimate business interests, such as support, maintenance, and security; and
- compliance with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

5. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Disclosure for Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:
- access to your data;
- correction of inaccurate data;
- deletion of your data;
- restriction of or objection to processing;
- data portability; and
- withdrawal of consent.

To exercise your rights, contact:
legal@bigbranestudios.com

We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.

8. Third-Party Services

We may link to, embed, or rely on third-party services that we do not control. Those third parties may collect, process, or store information under their own terms and privacy policies. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page and reflected by the “Last Updated” date at the top of the Policy.

10. Governing Law

This Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia, United States, excluding its conflict of laws rules.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Policy, contact:
legal@bigbranestudios.com
